About Us

Skytop Landscaping has been helping clients realize the potential of their exterior spaces since 2010.

We all know life moves fast and putting aside several hours each week for maintaining your lawn can be nearly impossible. This is why we have a number of customizable programs and services designed for you!

At Skytop Landscaping, we are passionate about helping you create the lawn and yard of your dreams. Whether you’re looking to improve your home or your commercial business site, we can help. We offer everything you need to landscape your lawn from the design to the building and installation itself.

Our professional experts at Skytop Landscaping are experienced in planning and designing lawns and are committed to excellent service. We are very personable and can work with your designs and plans as well as offering affordable design services. Together, we’ll create a lawn that is the envy of the neighborhood!
